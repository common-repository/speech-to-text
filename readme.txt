You can now change your speech to text that can be updated in your blog instantly without having to insert it using your keyboard.
== Description ==
1. A great way to transform your speech into text without actually typing the content. Although it will be working only in blog posts and not in web pages, it can surely help website owners to reduce their load of using keyboard. Just dictate it, and this WP plugin will write the content on your blog.
2. It will work just like  a writer without actually writing anything! You simply need to speak your thoughts and have access to a microphone, rest will be taken care of by this wonderful plugin. Simplifying and easing your task of inputting your thoughts through keyboards and punching alphabets! Say it, and see it written!
3. Without typing, it adds content to your website just through speech.
4. Chrome is necessary for the functioning of this plugin.
5. Microphone is the hardware required

== Installation ==

1. Copy the entire /speech-to-text/ directory into your /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin.
3. Posts have one new section called "Speech to Text".
4. Attached Microphone and then perform.
